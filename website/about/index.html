About

JOH was born from a passion to share the mindset and discipline of boxing. In Dutch, “Joh” is a casual expression meaning obviously, and that’s exactly our goal: to make boxing feel natural and accessible to everyone. What started as a simple training session, is now giving everyday people the tools to build confidence, discipline, and energetic self-expression. Today, JOH. offers boxing sessions in Amsterdam, providing 1-on-1 training, small group classes, and soon tailored programs for organizations.
